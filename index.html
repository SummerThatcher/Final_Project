About me

Hi, I'm Summer Thatcher and I'm just a self-proclaimed nerd who enjoys video games, outdoor activities like kayaking and ATVs, traveling and over the past couple years DIY projects. Like everyone else in quarantine I decided I needed to pick up more hobbies, for some it was gardening or baking but for me it was DIY projects. If you'd like you can check out a few of the projects. On February 6th 2022 I will have been with Ford Credit for 15 years. In that time I went from an entry level representative to an expert in my field of work, so much so that I've been trusted to head the training for my entire department for representatives and supervisors alike.







I want to be a software engineer because I enjoy problem solving and creating things like in my DIY projects. I have an eye for detail and utilizing that skill in a new way is an exciting notion. I love being able to work remotely from anywhere while also collaborating with a team and potentially do something that will impact a large number of people. The first time I ever played around with HTML and CSS I was 12 and I can remeber feeling so cool that with just a few strokes on the keyboard I could turn something drab and boring into something fun that represented me and my personality.
